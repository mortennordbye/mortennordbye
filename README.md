## 👋 About

I build and run automated, secure infrastructure for regulated industries. Day to day that means Azure-native platforms, Kubernetes (AKS and on-prem Talos), GitOps with ArgoCD, and Terraform-based CI/CD. Most of my work has been in healthcare, aviation and betting, where F5 WAF, SIEM and IDS integrations exist to meet strict compliance and availability requirements.

Currently a Cloud Engineer at [Orange Business](https://www.orange-business.com/), placed on customer accounts under a consultancy model. I took over architect responsibility on a betting-platform Azure migration, moved roughly 30 microservices onto AKS at peaks above 33M requests/day, and built the observability stack from scratch against the AMBA baseline.

Off the clock I run a six-node Talos Kubernetes cluster at home as an open lab, everything reconciled from Git. I learn in public and keep the configs and experiments on GitHub.

## 🚀 What I'm working on

- 🏗️ Architecting Azure platforms end to end — vWAN, Front Door, AKS, ACR, Managed Grafana — all in Terraform
- ☸️ Running GitOps at scale with ArgoCD app-of-apps, Helm and Kustomize
- 📈 Building observability stacks: Prometheus, Grafana, Loki and the OpenTelemetry Collector
- 🔐 Policy-as-code (EPAC), External Secrets Operator, cert-manager and Falco for hardened clusters
- 🧪 Breaking and rebuilding my [homelab](https://github.com/mortennordbye/Homelab) so production never has to be the experiment
